We Are Noxus team

We are Nexus, a team dedicated to developing innovative technology solutions to tackle challenges in various industries, especially in education, transportation, tourism, restaurants, and hotels. We provide software development, website design, and smart management systems. With extensive experience, we have developed projects across multiple sectors, including attendance systems, e-commerce platforms, and educational portals that make learning more accessible. We are also the creators of Anaconda Bus, a system that achieved great success this year, and Moshrefy, which expanded to 63 educational centers across Egypt in 2023. We believe in continuous growth and are always looking for creative and talented individuals to join our team. Our vision is to use technology to serve communities by developing innovative projects in AI, IoT, and e-learning systems. Let’s build the future together!
